About ProgressShelf

A personal progress tracker — built to give you a clear picture of what you're working on, and how far you've come.

Works instantly across devices under the same account — no refresh needed.

Three ways to track

  • Goals — set a deadline, update progress as you go
  • Checklists — tick things off, and your progress bar updates automatically
  • Quick Notes — write freely, with Markdown formatting support (bold, italics, links, and more)

Deadlines that show themselves

Every card can carry a deadline, visualized as a draining border that shifts color as time runs out — so you feel your timeline, not just read it.

Notifications, your way

Set an alert at an exact deadline, a fixed time before it, or as a percentage of time remaining — whatever matches how you actually think about time.

Built to bend

Nothing is locked in once saved — edit any card, any field, at any stage. Input fields are forgiving too: type 1.5 in an hours field and it's read as 1 hr 30 min.

Search that actually searches

Global search looks inside your cards, not just their titles — though titles are still prioritized first.

Everywhere you need it

Install ProgressShelf as an app on Windows, Mac, Android, or iPhone.
